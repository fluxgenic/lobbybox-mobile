Privacy Policy

FluxGenic is committed to protecting resident, guard, and visitor information. This policy outlines how we collect, store, and use data across our platform.

Information we collect

We collect parcel images, delivery details, resident notifications, and guard activity logs to deliver our concierge services. Optional integrations may collect vehicle plate or visitor information when enabled by customers.

How we use data

Data is processed to authenticate users, deliver notifications, generate analytics, and improve AI accuracy. FluxGenic never sells customer data and only shares information with subprocessors required to operate the platform.

Storage and retention

All data is hosted in Malaysian data centres with encryption at rest. Parcel images and logs are retained based on customer-configured retention settings and permanently deleted when the subscription ends.

Resident rights

Residents may request access, correction, or deletion of their data by contacting their property administrator. FluxGenic supports PDPA-aligned workflows to fulfil these requests promptly.

Contact

Privacy-related questions can be directed to info@fluxgenic.com.
